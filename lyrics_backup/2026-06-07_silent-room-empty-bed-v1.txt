[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Shadows dancing on the wall, the night is fading slow
I still can feel your magic where the amber embers glow
You left a trace of summer rain upon the empty bed
Now every little whisper is echoing in my head

[Verse 1]
The midnight moon is pouring through the open window pane
Painting pictures of the moments that we never could contain
I trace the fading outlines of the place where you would lie
Beneath the quiet canopy of an endless starry sky

[Pre-Chorus]
The temperature is rising in the middle of the dark
I only need your presence just to light another spark
I hear your whisper lingering upon the midnight breeze
It pulls me under like a tide upon the deepest seas

[Chorus]
In this silent room and empty bed I am reaching out for you
With a heavy, beating, tangled heart I know you feel it too
I want your hands upon my skin, the fire in your stare
I am drowning in the beautiful illusion you are there

[Post-Chorus]
(I am reaching out for you)
Come and light this dark again and make the shadows new

[Verse 2]
The coffee cup is cooling on the little bedside stand
I still remember how it felt to hold your gentle hand
A silent conversation that we spoke without a word
The loudest kind of melody that I have ever heard

[Pre-Chorus 2]

[Chorus]

[Post-Chorus 2]
(I am reaching out for you)

[Bridge]
The hours bleed together when the dawn begins to break
A heavy kind of longing that my lonely heart will take
I close my eyes and feel you like a gentle, summer rain
And even Saeed surrenders to the beauty of this pain
So pull me back into the fire where we belong tonight
And wrap me in the shadows till the early morning light

[Spoken]
